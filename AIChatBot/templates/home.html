Welcome to Shane Cooke's Website

The purpose of this website is to show off the functionality of my AI powered ChatBot.

How it works!

Step 1: Open the chatbot with the comment/chat button in the bottom right of the webpage.
Step 2: Type your question into the input field (Where it says 'Enter your question....').
Step 3: Hit 'Enter' on your keyboard or click the 'Send' button.
Step 4: Using OpenAI's API, an embedding of your question is created. This embedding is then compared to the database of questions, and if it matches one of them, the answer is returned to you. If there is no match, a custom message with show instead.

Some of the questions to ask:

  • Do you have a refund policy?
  • What leverage is available?
  • Do I trade with real funds?
